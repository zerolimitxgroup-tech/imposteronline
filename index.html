HOW TO PLAY

1. Add Players
Local: enter names on one device. Online: everyone joins a room.

2. Pick a Category
Choose from Animals, Food, Movies, Sports, and more.

3. See Your Word
Crew members see the same word. The Imposter sees IMPOSTER — but gets a similar hint word from the same category to help them bluff!

4. Give Clues
Take turns giving a one-word clue related to the secret word. Goes around for multiple rounds. The Imposter must bluff using their hint!

5. Vote
After all clue rounds, everyone votes on who they think the Imposter is.

6. Scoring
Crew earns 10pts for catching. Imposter earns 15pts for surviving!
