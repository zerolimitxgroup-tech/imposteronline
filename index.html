HOW TO PLAY

1. Add Players
Enter 3–20 player names. Pass the device around!

2. Pick a Category
Choose from Animals, Food, Movies, Sports, and more.

3. See Your Word
Crew sees the same word. Imposter sees IMPOSTER + a similar hint word.

4. Give Clues
Take turns saying a one-word clue. The Imposter must bluff!

5. Vote
Everyone votes on who they think the Imposter is.

6. Scoring
Crew +10pts for catching. Imposter +15pts for surviving!
